On the upside, you also get a bugfix included that was originally only scheduled to go out with 1.6.0. Due to this being a hotfix release the [changelog](https://github.com/OctoPrint/OctoPrint/releases/tag/1.5.2) is very short and only consists of these entries: > * [#3855](https://github.com/OctoPrint/OctoPrint/issues/3855) & [#3867](https://github.com/OctoPrint/OctoPrint/issues/3867) - Fix settings merging on the frontend causing issues with array values. > * Pin `watchdog` dependency to 0.10.4 to work around backwards incompatibility with the just release 0.10.5 under Python 3.
